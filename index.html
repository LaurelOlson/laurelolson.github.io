VanHackHERS

We are a group of self-identified woman web developers and tech professionals determined to do good while juggling the everyday roller coaster of full-time jobs, pets, relationships, and side hustles. We are united in our passion to contribute to our communities in tangible ways, to uplift marginalized groups, to encourage young women to get into tech, and to promote professional women within the tech industry.

We care a lot about this stuff, because it matters. If you also care, let us help you.
